Poster Session and Elevator Talk

The poster session will be held on the first day of the symposium, November 12, 7:30 pm to 9 pm. The format is rather casual. The goal of the poster session is for you to showcase your work and to foster discussion with fellow symposium attendees.

We also encourage you to sign up for elevator talk when submitting your poster abstract. The elevator talk is on the same date, from 6:30 to 7 pm. For those selected, you will give a 3-minute presentation to highlight your research.

TTBA Symposium now offers an extended abstract submission deadline. The goal is to enrich the symposium with more diverse studies and facilitate more conversations. The extended deadline for submission is October 22, 2016, 11:59 a.m. Central Time.

Guidlines:

  1. The poster holder's size is 8 feet (96 inches) in width and 4 feet (48 inches) in height.
    The maximum dimensions for each poster is 48 inches by 48 inches.
  2. Extended submission deadline: October 22, 2016, 11:59 a.m. Central Time.
  3. Abstracts submitted after the general submission deadline are not eligible for elevator talk.
  4. General submission deadline: October 16, 2016.
  5. Decisions for elevator talk will be sent by October 25, 2016.
  6. You may updated your abstract before the deadline.
